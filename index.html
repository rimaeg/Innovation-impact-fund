How We Operate — Quick Guide

At the Innovation and Impact Fund, our primary mission is to create real opportunities for immigrants and communities across U.S. cities. We work through a transparent, inclusive, and results-driven process to ensure that every grant we give has a measurable impact.

  1. Confirm eligibility & read the solicitation.
  2. Register your organization (EIN, SAM if federal), prepare budget & milestones.
  3. Submit summary abstract and implementation plan before deadline.
  4. Selected applicants submit detailed proposal and performance metrics.
  5. Occasionally run internal checks and distribute grants accordingly to citizens.
